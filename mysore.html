About Mysore Tourism :

Mysore , officially Mysuru (Kannada: [ˈmaɪˈsuːɾu] ), is a metropolitan city in the southern Indian state of Karnataka. It is the state's third-most populous and is one of the cleanest cities in India according to Swachh Survekshan. It is the seat of the Wadiyar dynasty and was the capital of the Kingdom of Mysore
